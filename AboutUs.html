    Our Story

    In 2019, Sapan noticed classmates struggling with assignments. Between juggling classes, jobs, and life, completing tasks on time felt like a constant battle. Fueled by coffee and a desire to help, they launched a small online platform offering assignment guidance. It started simple - clear explanations and personalized support. Word spread. Soon, more students found their way to Sapan's platform, now called Uniment. They built a team of passionate tutors, each a master in their field. Uniment wasn't just about completing assignments; it was about understanding. Today, Uniment thrives. It's a testament to the power of friendship, a cup of coffee, and a simple idea that blossomed into a supportive community for students everywhere.
